Delivery FAQs

Common questions.

Do you deliver on Sundays?

No, we do not offer delivery on Sundays. The canteen is open for eat-in on Sundays but delivery is Monday to Saturday only.

Can I pre-order for a specific time?

Yes. When you call or submit the order form, just let us know your preferred delivery time and we will do our best to accommodate it.

What if I'm outside the EX17 area?

We currently only deliver within the EX17 postcode area. If you're just outside, give us a call and we'll see what we can do — we're always happy to help where possible.

Can I add special instructions?

Absolutely. Whether it's dietary requirements, sauce preferences or specific cooking requests — just mention it when you order and we'll do our best to accommodate.

Is there a maximum order size?

No maximum. For large group or office orders we recommend calling ahead so we can allocate enough preparation time to ensure everything arrives fresh and hot.

How do I pay for my delivery order?

We accept cash or card on delivery. Our driver carries a card reader so contactless payment is available. We do not take payment in advance for standard orders.
