Our story · Crediton

Good food starts with good people.

Corner Canteen was born from a simple idea — that everyone in Crediton deserves access to honest, freshly cooked food without the fuss. From our corner on Park Street, we serve the community one plate at a time.

Jalf Fletcher Owner & Head Cook · Corner Canteen
Meet the owner

Hi, I'm Jalf — and I cook every single dish myself.

I started Corner Canteen because I wanted to give Crediton something it was missing — a proper local canteen where you could walk in, sit down, and eat something genuinely good without spending a fortune or waiting forever.

I'm 28 years old and I've been cooking professionally since I was 19. Before opening here at 8 Park Street, I worked in kitchens across Devon learning how to make simple food taste exceptional. The focus has always been the same: fresh ingredients, honest recipes, and cooking with care.

"There's nothing complicated about good food. You just have to care about what you're serving and who you're serving it to."

Today we serve breakfasts, hot mains, sides and desserts Monday to Saturday — and we deliver across the whole EX17 area. Whether you're a regular who pops in every morning or someone ordering lunch from the office, you'll always get the same quality plate.

What we stand for

Our values.

Everything we do at Corner Canteen comes back to these three things.

Freshness above everything

Every item on the menu is cooked fresh that day. We don't freeze, we don't reheat, and we don't serve anything we wouldn't be proud to eat ourselves.

Community first

We're part of Crediton, not just a business in it. We source locally where we can, support our neighbours, and try to be the kind of place people are genuinely glad exists.

Honest pricing

Good food shouldn't cost a fortune. We keep our prices fair because we want everyone in Crediton to be able to afford a proper hot meal without it being a treat.

Find us
